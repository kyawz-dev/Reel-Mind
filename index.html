HOW IT EDITS

One upload. A full editorial pass.

00:00

Reads the footage

Transcribes speech, tracks faces and energy, detects silence, filler words, false starts, and topic changes across the whole take.

00:14

Writes an edit plan

Marks the hook, flags slow stretches, plans zooms, B-roll slots, and subtitle style — and tells you why, in plain language, before it cuts.

00:29

Cuts, scores, subtitles

Assembles the timeline, grades color, ducks music to your voice, and scores the result on hook strength, pacing, and retention.

00:41

You refine it in chat

"Cut the second story shorter." "Fewer zooms." "More like my last upload." The AI re-edits only what you asked — nothing else moves.
